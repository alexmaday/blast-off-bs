  • You are a whisper

    • By Alex Maday on August 20, 2017

    • 2 comments

    • JavaScript Robots

    Let's go up in here, and start having some fun I started painting as a hobby when I was little. I didn't know I had any talent. I believe talent is just a pursued interest. Anybody can do what I do. Absolutely no pressure. You are just a whisper floating across

    a mountain. Each highlight must have it's own private shadow. I sincerely wish for you every possible joy life could bring.

    Now it's beginning to make a little sense. There isn't a rule. You just practice and find out which way works best for you. You can spend all day playing with mountains. We don't make mistakes we just have happy little accidents. If you don't like it - change it. It's your world.

    Volunteering your time; it pays you and your whole community fantastic dividends. Just let this happen. We just let this flow right out of our minds. You can do anything here - the only pre-requisite is that it makes you happy.

    Anything you want to do you can do here. If you hypnotize it, it will go away. Be careful. You can always add more - but you can't take it away. There's not a thing in the world wrong with washing your brush.
